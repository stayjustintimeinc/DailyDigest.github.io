🌎 WORLD NEWS

U.S. announces military drills with Guyana amid dispute over oil-rich region with Venezuela

-CBS News

1. The United States announced joint military flight drills in Guyana amid tensions over a disputed oil-rich region with Venezuela.

2. Guyana is preparing to defend itself and its borders against a potential invasion from Venezuela, but hopes for a peaceful resolution through diplomacy.

3. The international community, including the U.S. and Brazil, is becoming increasingly concerned about the escalating conflict and its implications for regional peace and security.

Reuters releases investigation finding Israeli tank fire killed 1 of its journalists

-NPR

1. Investigations by news organizations and human rights groups suggest that an Israeli tank round killed a Reuters video journalist and wounded several others near the Lebanese border in October.

2. The reports indicate that Israeli forces either knew or should have known that they were targeting journalists, contradicting the Israeli military's repeated denial of intentionally targeting journalists.

3. The ongoing conflict between Israel and Hamas has resulted in the deaths of at least 63 journalists and media workers, making it one of the deadliest conflicts for media workers in recent history.

Alberto Fujimori, Peru’s divisive former president, released from jail

-The Guardian

1. Former Peruvian leader Alberto Fujimori was released from prison after a court ruling granted him a humanitarian pardon, despite international objections.

2. Fujimori, who served a 25-year sentence for human rights abuses and corruption, was greeted by his lawyer and his children, Kenji and Keiko Fujimori, among others.

3. The decision to release Fujimori has sparked controversy and criticism, with the Inter-American Court of Human Rights urging Peru to delay his release and human rights organizations expressing concerns about accountability.

🏦 BUSINESS NEWS

McDonald's new CosMc's chain could put 'valuation cap' on Dutch Bros despite small start

-OregonLive

1. McDonald's unveils CosMc's, a new drive-thru beverage chain, with plans to open 10 locations.

2. The chain, positioned to challenge Dutch Bros, will initially focus on one location near McDonald's headquarters and nine in Texas.

3. CosMc's will serve colorful beverages with food items, aiming to address the "3 p.m. slump" and evaluate expansion potential based on initial results.

The OpenAI Board Member Who Clashed With Sam Altman Shares Her Side

-The Wall Street Journal

1. Helen Toner, a board member of OpenAI, played a role in the ousting and subsequent return of CEO Sam Altman.

2. Toner has become a symbol of the tension between AI-safety advocates and those prioritizing technological progress.

3. The situation highlights the ongoing debate surrounding the balance between AI advancements and potential risks.

Lululemon shares fall as retailer gives tepid holiday outlook despite strong start to shopping season

-CNBC

1. Lululemon reported strong third-quarter demand and a positive start to the holiday shopping season.

2. Net income for the quarter was $249 million, compared to $255 million in the previous year.

3. The company's holiday guidance fell short of expectations, with projected sales and earnings lower than analysts' estimates.

🤖 TECH NEWS

Alphabet soars as Wall Street cheers arrival of AI model Gemini

-Reuters

1. Alphabet shares rose 5.3% as Wall Street welcomed the launch of Gemini, an artificial intelligence model that can process different forms of media.

2. Gemini is faster than OpenAI's latest model and is expected to help Alphabet regain its leadership in AI research.

3. Alphabet plans to integrate Gemini into its Bard chatbot and release an advanced version early next year.

WhatsApp is rolling out self-destructing voice messages

-The Verge

1. WhatsApp is introducing a "View Once" feature for voice messages, allowing them to disappear after they have been listened to.

2. This feature was already available for photos and videos, but now it is expanding to cover audio messages as well.

3. The company suggests various use cases for this feature, including sharing sensitive information or planning surprises.

Meta Adds AI Across Its Apps, Launches Standalone Image Generator

-Decrypt

1. Meta AI is adding over 20 new AI-driven features to its apps, making it more helpful and accurate in generating responses and summaries.

2. Meta is launching Imagine as a standalone image generator, competing with top models in the industry.

3. Meta is implementing invisible watermarking on AI-generated images to promote transparency and address concerns about the misuse of AI in content generation.
