FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

On this day, December 28th, in 1895, the Lumière brothers held the first ever public screening of projected motion pictures in Paris, marking the birth of cinema as we know it today. Their screening took place at the Salon Indien du Grand Café, where they showcased short films such as "Sortie de l'usine Lumière à Lyon," capturing everyday scenes from around France. The event astonished the audience, who had never witnessed moving images on such a grand scale before. This momentous occasion paved the way for the revolutionary art form that continues to captivate and entertain audiences worldwide!

Mortgage rates drop again, but pending home sales stall

-Fox Business

1. Mortgage rates have decreased, providing hope for prospective buyers. The average rate for the benchmark 30-year fixed mortgage fell to 6.61% this week, while the rate on the 15-year fixed mortgage dropped to 5.93%.

2. However, pending home sales have remained flat at a record low in November. This indicates that the decline in mortgage rates has not been enough to stimulate the housing market.

3. Housing starts, on the other hand, surged 14.8% last month, suggesting some progress in the stagnant market. Buyers have been leaning towards new construction due to limited existing home supply and builders offering incentives with lower rates.

Google agrees to settle Chrome incognito mode class action lawsuit

-Ars Technica

1. Google is ready to settle a class-action lawsuit filed in 2020 over its Chrome browser's Incognito mode.
