The world's most polluted megacity is trying to clean up its air : Goats and Soda

-NPR

1. Residents of New Delhi are estimated to lose about 12 years of life due to air pollution, according to a report by the Air Quality Life Index.

2. New Delhi is considered the world's most polluted megacity, with over half a billion residents living in the region.

3. Despite efforts to reduce pollution, such as promoting electric vehicles and implementing real-time monitoring stations, the measures taken so far have not been sufficient to address the severe air pollution in the city.

Israel Gaza: Hamas raped and mutilated women on 7 October, BBC hears

-BBC.com

UK interior minister signs new Rwanda treaty to resurrect asylum plan

-Reuters UK

3. Implementation of the treaty is expected to face political and legal challenges, and deportation flights may be delayed until after the upcoming election.
