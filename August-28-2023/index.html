French ban of abaya robes in schools draws applause, criticism

-Reuters

1. The French government has banned children from wearing the abaya, a full-length robe worn by some Muslim women, in state-run schools.

2. The ban received support from conservative groups who view it as a necessary measure to uphold secularism and prevent breaches of religious neutrality in schools.

3. However, the ban also faced criticism from the hard-left and some Muslim representatives who argue that it infringes on religious freedom and unfairly targets the Muslim community.

Chinese carmaker BYD to buy US firm Jabil's mobility business for $2.2 bln

-Reuters

1. Chinese automaker BYD's electronics unit has agreed to buy the mobile electronics manufacturing business of U.S.-based manufacturer Jabil for 15.8 billion yuan ($2.2 billion) in order to expand its customer base and capture potential growth in the sector.

2. BYD Electronic's major business is selling electronic components for consumer electronic products like smartphones and laptops, accounting for more than 70% of its total revenue in 2022.

3. The deal is expected to enhance BYD's overall competitiveness and support its long-term sustainable development, while allowing Jabil to invest further in electric vehicles, renewable energy, healthcare, AI cloud data centers, and other end-markets.
