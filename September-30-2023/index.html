🌎 WORLD NEWS

Liberals lead in Slovakia election, exit poll shows

-POLITICO Europe

1. Progressive Slovakia, led by Michal Šimečka, is leading in Slovakia's election with 23.5% of the vote, followed by Robert Fico's Smer with 21.9%.

2. The front-runner, Michal Šimečka, has a strong chance of forming a coalition and becoming the next prime minister.

3. Potential partners for Progressive Slovakia include OĽaNO, Sloboda a solidarita, and the Christian Democrats, which would give them a majority in parliament.

Pope Francis appoints 21 new cardinals — including an American — to help reform Catholic Church

-New York Post

1. Pope Francis appointed 21 new cardinals, focusing on diversity, ahead of a meeting to discuss controversial issues such as LGBTQ+ followers, women's roles in the church, and celibacy. The new cardinals hail from various countries including the US, France, Argentina, South Africa, and Hong Kong.

2. The appointment of Victor Manuel Fernandez, the new head of the Vatican's doctrine office, sparked controversy due to his handling of a case involving sexual abuse by a priest in Argentina. Pope Francis clarified that Fernandez would not deal with sexual assault cases as a cardinal.

3. New cardinals Robert Prevost and Christophe Pierre, who are responsible for vetting bishop candidates and overseeing investigations, indicate a potential balance-of-power shift in the US, where conservative bishops have criticized Pope Francis' reforms. The pope will also host a synod to discuss women's roles, LGBTQ+ issues, and priestly celibacy with bishops and lay members.

French government combats bedbug epidemic in Paris, urges calm

-Fox News

1. The French government is urging calm as it tackles an outbreak of bedbugs in Paris.

2. Recent videos have shown bedbugs infesting public transportation and other areas of daily life in the city.

3. The government launched an initiative three years ago to inform residents about bedbug spread and offer resources for dealing with them.

🏦 BUSINESS NEWS

All Onewheel e-skateboards are recalled after reported deaths

-NPR

1. Future Motion is recalling all models of its Onewheel electric skateboards after four deaths and multiple injuries were linked to the products.

2. The reported deaths were a result of head trauma, with at least three of the victims not wearing a helmet.

3. Future Motion is implementing a new safety feature, a "haptic buzz" alert function, to help riders identify when they may be in danger of crashing. This update will be available for certain models within the next six weeks.

UBS says reports of DOJ probe into Russia sanctions evasion are wrong

-CNN

1. UBS denies awareness of a probe by the US Department of Justice into alleged compliance-related missteps by its subsidiary Credit Suisse.

2. UBS has been in contact with the DOJ regarding the reported probe.

3. The Swiss Bankers Association estimates over $200 billion in Russian money has been kept in Swiss banks.

3 things that will impact stocks going into the first trading week of the fourth quarter

-CNBC

1. Wall Street closed out the third quarter with declines in the Dow, S&P 500, and Nasdaq.

2. The third-quarter declines broke three straight quarters of gains for the Dow and S&P 500.

3. Investors are hoping for a strong performance in the final quarter of the year.

🤖 TECH NEWS

Apple identifies issues causing overheating in the iPhone 15

-Reuters

1. Apple has identified issues causing new iPhones to run warmer than expected, including a bug in the iOS 17 software, which will be fixed in an upcoming update.

2. The increased background activity during the device setup or restoration process may cause the new iPhones to feel warmer in the first few days.

3. Some recent updates to third-party apps, such as Asphalt 9, Instagram, and Uber, are overloading the system and causing the devices to heat up. Apple is working with app developers to fix the issue.

Google Pixel 8 Pro unboxing images turn up in latest leak

-Notebookcheck.net

1. The Google Pixel 8 Pro has suffered another image-related leak.

2. The leaked images show the phone undergoing quality check in a factory setting.

3. The images reveal the Obsidian Black and Porcelain color options of the Pixel 8 Pro, along with its accessories.

'Counterfeit people': The dangers posed by Meta's AI celebrity lookalike chatbots

-FRANCE 24 English

1. Meta has announced the launch of chatbots with personalities resembling certain celebrities, aiming to entertain younger users.

2. These chatbots have faces and are associated with celebrities such as Paris Hilton, Charli D'Amelio, and Naomi Osaka.
