Earhart completed this historic journey in 18 hours and 15 minutes, solidifying herself as a pioneering figure in aviation. 🌎 WORLD NEWS

Explosion at historic Texas hotel injures 21 and scatters debris in downtown Fort Worth

-CTV News

1. An explosion occurred at a historic hotel in Fort Worth, Texas, injuring 21 people, with one in critical condition.

2. The explosion blew out windows and scattered debris onto downtown streets, with several people trapped in the basement of the hotel.

3. Authorities believe a gas leak caused the blast, and investigations are underway to determine the exact cause.

Senior Hezbollah commander killed in Israeli airstrike in Lebanon

-CBC News

1. An Israeli airstrike in southern Lebanon killed an elite Hezbollah commander, escalating tensions in the border area.

2. The fighting in both Gaza and the Israeli-Lebanese border has raised fears of another Mideast war.

3. The situation in Gaza is dire, with thousands of Palestinians killed and injured, hospitals struggling to cope, and widespread destruction and displacement.

At least 79 journalists killed in Israel-Hamas war: experts

-CTV News

1. At least 79 journalists and media workers have died in Gaza, Israel, and Lebanon since the start of the Israel-Hamas war on October 7, 2023.

2. Journalists have been dying in this war at a rate of about one per day, which is unprecedented and higher than previous conflicts like the Vietnam War and the war in Yugoslavia.

3. Media workers killed in Gaza have included not only those working for independent outlets but also those affiliated with both Hamas and the Palestinian Authority. Many have died in Israeli airstrikes on homes and public spaces, while others were shot.

🏦 BUSINESS NEWS

Air Transat, union agree on new tentative deal for flight attendants

-Global News

1. Air Transat and the Canadian Union of Public Employees have reached a new tentative agreement for the airline's 2,100 flight attendants.

2. The previous agreement, reached in December, was rejected by the flight attendants, but details of the new deal are not yet available.

3. If approved, the proposed agreement will be in place for the next five years and will cover the flight attendants based in Montreal and Toronto.

United Airlines says loose bolts, other 'installation issues' found in grounded Boeing 737 Max 9 jets

-CBC News

1. United Airlines has discovered loose bolts and other "installation issues" on the plugs used to seal emergency doors on some Boeing 737 Max 9 jets after an inspection prompted by a similar incident on an Alaska Airlines jet.

2. The U.S. Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has grounded all Max 9s operated by Alaska and United, as well as some flown by foreign airlines, following a mid-flight blowout on Alaska Airlines Flight 1282. No serious injuries were reported.

3. The FAA has approved guidelines for inspecting and repairing the door plugs on other Max 9 jets, potentially speeding up the return to service for the grounded planes. Alaska owns 64 Max 9s, while United Airlines owns 79.

🤖 TECH NEWS

Apple's first Vision Pro ad echoes the original iPhone commercial

-The Verge

1. Apple released the first ad for its Vision Pro headset, featuring a montage of clips from movies and TV shows showcasing characters putting on goggles, helmets, and masks.

2. The ad structure is reminiscent of Apple's original iPhone commercial, which featured clips of people answering telephones.

3. Preorders for the Vision Pro will begin on January 19th, with official availability starting on February 2nd. Demos of the headset will be offered at Apple retail stores.

Samsung Electronics Launches 2024 Neo QLED, MICRO LED, OLED and Lifestyle Displays To Spark the AI Screen Era and New Ways of Life

-Samsung

1. Samsung announces its latest QLED, MICRO LED, OLED, and Lifestyle display lineups along with a new AI processor, focusing on improving picture and sound quality and providing AI-powered features.

2. The Neo QLED 8K lineup offers improved picture quality with its NQ8 AI Gen3 processor and enhanced audio quality, as well as personalized content and service experience through the 2024 Tizen OS.

3. Samsung introduces Samsung Daily+, a hub for in-home activity, offering various services and features for seamless connectivity between devices, as well as enhanced compatibility and accessibility offerings in their latest TV and screen lineup.

Nvidia debuts RTX 40 Super chips to power gaming and AI efforts at CES 2024

-Yahoo Canada Finance

1. Nvidia has introduced its GeForce RTX 40 Super series of desktop graphics cards at CES 2024, targeting gaming and AI applications.

2. The RTX 4080 Super is 1.4x faster than the RTX 3080 Ti, while the RTX 4070 Ti Super is 1.6x faster than the RTX 3070 Ti, with even greater performance gains when using Nvidia's AI-powered Deep Learning Super Sampling (DLSS).

3. Nvidia joins other tech companies in promoting AI PCs, which allow users to run AI applications on their own devices without uploading private data to the cloud. Intel and AMD are waiting for developers to create programs that leverage AI PC features.
