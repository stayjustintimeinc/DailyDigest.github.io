Orsted, Offshore Wind Firm, Cancels N.J. Projects

-The New York Times

1. Orsted, a leading offshore wind farm developer, is scrapping plans for two wind farms off the coast of New Jersey, resulting in write-offs of up to $5.6 billion.

2. The shakeout in the offshore wind industry in the United States is hampering the Biden administration's efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and rely on renewable energy.

3. Factors such as high inflation, soaring interest rates, lack of protection from inflation in early contracts, and delays in approvals during the Trump administration contribute to the challenges faced by offshore wind developers in the United States.

Why a Fed rate pause won't give Biden a break

-POLITICO

1. The U.S. government has been borrowing a significant amount of money ($1 trillion last quarter alone) and will continue to need even more, which will be obtained at higher interest rates.

2. Higher borrowing costs could negatively impact the economy, particularly as the election year begins, putting pressure on President Biden and Democrats in Congress to reduce deficits.

3. The U.S. Treasury's borrowing plans have garnered attention, and rising yields are attributed to increased supply and fewer buyers, including a reduction in the Fed's holdings of Treasury securities. However, not all experts are alarmed by the debt, suggesting it is easier to control now than under previous administrations.

WeWork shares sink to record low on reports bankruptcy filing imminent

-Reuters

1. WeWork shares plummeted nearly 50% to a record low amid reports of an impending bankruptcy filing.

2. The company, once valued at $47 billion, now has a market capitalization of only $121 million.

3. WeWork's struggles stem from its heavy debt load and failure to turn a profit, leading to skepticism over its business model.

First Benchmark Results Surface for M3 Chip in New Macs

-MacRumors

1. The M3 chip has achieved single-core and multi-core scores of around 3,000 and 11,700, respectively, making it up to 20% faster than the M2 chip.
