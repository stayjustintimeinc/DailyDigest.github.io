What to know about North Korea's spy satellite launch

-Reuters

1. North Korea has successfully launched its first spy satellite, Chollima-1, with assistance likely from Russia.

Before Altman's Ouster, OpenAI's Board Was Divided and Feuding

-The New York Times

1. Sam Altman, the former CEO of OpenAI, was involved in conflicts with board members over a research paper critical of the company and concerns about A.I. safety.

Dow rises more than 100 points as markets head into Thanksgiving holiday: Live updates

3. Consumer staples and small- and mid-cap stocks outperformed, while the energy sector lost ground after OPEC delayed a meeting on production cuts.

Anthropic Upgrades Claude With Nearly Twice The Capabilities of GPT-4 Turbo

-Decrypt

1. Anthropic has released Claude 2.1, a language model with a 200,000-token context window, surpassing the capabilities of GPT-4 Turbo.

The AI startup behind Stable Diffusion is now testing generative video

-Engadget

1. Stability AI has released Stable Video Diffusion, a new product that allows users to create video from a single image using generative AI technology.

3. Stable Video Diffusion is currently available for research purposes only, with potential applications in sectors such as advertising, education, and entertainment.
