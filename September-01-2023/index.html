FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

On this day in history, September 1st, in 1923, the famous Hollywood sign was officially dedicated. Originally reading "Hollywoodland," this iconic landmark was initially created as an advertising campaign for a local real estate development. It was only meant to last for about 18 months, but due to its immense popularity, it remained and became a symbol of the American entertainment industry. In 1949, the "land" part of the sign was removed, making it the well-known Hollywood sign we recognize today.
