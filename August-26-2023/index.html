Fed chair warns US inflation battle not over and hints at further rate rises

-The Guardian

1. Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell warns that the fight against inflation in the US is not over and that interest rates may need to rise further to control it.

2. Despite a decline in the annual rate of inflation, prices of essential items such as food, housing, and gas remain higher than pre-pandemic levels.

3. Powell emphasizes that the Fed's goal is to bring inflation down to the 2% target and is prepared to raise rates further if necessary, while being cautious about the potential impact on the economy.

Windows 11 gets native RAR support, here is how it compares to WinRAR and other apps

-Neowin

1. Microsoft plans to release a big feature update for Windows 11, version 23H2, which includes a redesigned File Explorer and native support for RAR, TAR, 7z, and other file compression formats.

2. The updated File Explorer's performance in extracting TAR archives is comparable to WinRAR and NanaZIP, all finishing in about 80 seconds. However, it significantly lags behind in extracting RAR and 7z archives, taking almost six minutes and nine minutes respectively.

3. While the native support for additional file compression formats is a positive addition, the updated File Explorer lacks certain conveniences and features, such as a status bar, support for password-protected archives, and the ability to pack files into formats other than ZIP. It is unlikely to replace dedicated apps like WinRAR for users frequently working with archives.
