FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

On this day in history, October 21st, in 1805, the Battle of Trafalgar took place off the coast of Spain during the Napoleonic Wars. This naval battle between the British Royal Navy, commanded by Admiral Lord Nelson, and the combined fleets of France and Spain, resulted in a decisive victory for the British. It is particularly notable as Lord Nelson led his fleet to victory despite being fatally wounded by a sniper during the battle. His famous last words were, "Thank God I have done my duty."
