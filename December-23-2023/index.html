🌎 WORLD NEWS

Russian politician Yekaterina Duntsova faces roadblock in presidential campaign

-NPR

1. Russian politician Yekaterina Duntsova, who is calling for peace in Ukraine, faces obstacles in her presidential campaign as the Central Election Commission refuses to accept her nomination due to errors in the submitted documents.

2. Duntsova plans to appeal the decision in Russia's Supreme Court and asks the Yabloko political party to nominate her as a candidate since she cannot convene a second meeting of supporters.

3. Several Russian parties have announced their backing for candidates in the upcoming presidential election, with incumbent President Vladimir Putin expected to win.

Humanitarian crisis worsens in Gaza as Israel-Hamas war rages: Live updates

-CNN

1. Israel's ground forces have expanded their operations in the southern and northern Gaza Strip, engaging in fierce battles and seizing weapons and infrastructure from Hamas.

2. US President Joe Biden spoke with Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu about the objectives and phasing of the Israel-Hamas conflict and the importance of securing the release of hostages held by Hamas.

3. Aid trucks, including those from the Palestine Red Crescent Society and the Egyptian Red Crescent Society, have entered Gaza with water, food, relief supplies, and medical supplies. The number of trucks entering Gaza has significantly decreased since the start of the war.

Who are the Houthi rebels and why are they attacking Red Sea ships?

-BBC.com

1. Cargo ships in the Red Sea are being targeted by drone and rocket attacks by the Houthi rebel group in Yemen.

2. Major shipping companies are diverting vessels away from the Red Sea to avoid the attacks.

3. The US has launched an international naval operation to protect ships, and countries including the UK, Canada, France, Bahrain, Norway, and Spain have joined.

What stores are open and closed on Christmas Day in 2023? Hours for Walmart, Kroger, CVS and more

-NBC News

1. Many major chains, including Walmart, Target, Costco, CVS Pharmacy, Walgreens, and Starbucks, will be closed on Christmas Day.

'Santa Claus' rally time for stock market? Why investors should dial back their expectations for this seasonal year-end gift.

-MarketWatch

1. The Santa Claus rally, characterized by stock market gains in the last five trading days of the year and the first two trading days of the new year, is expected to boost the S&P 500 by an average of 1.3% based on historical data.

2. This year, the stock market has already experienced significant gains before the official Santa Claus rally period, leading some analysts to believe that the traditional rally may be somewhat subdued.

3. Despite concerns of the stock market being overbought and potential technical risks, analysts suggest not betting against the seasonal momentum and highlight the historical correlation between the Santa rally and positive stock market returns in January and the subsequent year.

Watch out for counterfeit Ozempic, FDA says

-NPR

1. Thousands of counterfeit units of the diabetes drug Ozempic have been seized in the legitimate supply chain, with some fake products still in circulation.

2. Novo Nordisk, the manufacturer of Ozempic, is working with the FDA to test the seized drugs but has not announced any quality or safety findings yet.

3. The FDA is warning wholesalers, pharmacies, healthcare practitioners, and patients to check their product labels for counterfeit units and report any questionable products.

Apple quietly released an open source multimodal LLM in October

-VentureBeat

2. Apple made breakthroughs in deploying LLMs on iPhones, introducing new techniques for 3D avatars and efficient language model inference, enabling immersive visual experiences and running complex AI systems on consumer devices.

Video Boost on the Google Pixel 8 Pro is a handy low-light video tool

-The Verge

1. Google introduced Night Sight for low-light photography on the Pixel 3, which was a game-changer. However, implementing a similar feature for video is more challenging.

2. Google's Video Boost with Night Sight uses AI to process videos, enhancing detail and color. However, the processing takes place in the cloud and can take hours for even a short video clip.

3. Video Boost is most effective in very low-light conditions, where it can restore lost color and detail. However, it is not as dramatic as the difference between regular and Night Sight photos. There is a sweet spot where Video Boost can be useful.
