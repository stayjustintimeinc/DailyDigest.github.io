Established by the will of Alfred Nobel, the inventor of dynamite, these prestigious prizes are awarded annually in recognition of outstanding achievements in various fields including physics, chemistry, medicine, literature, and peace. Over the years, the Nobel Prizes have become a symbol of excellence and innovation, fostering advancements that shape our world today!

🌎 WORLD NEWS

Argentine President Milei warns economic shock unavoidable in maiden speech

-Reuters Canada

1. Argentina's libertarian economist, Javier Milei, takes office and warns of the need for a sharp fiscal shock to address the country's economic crisis.

2. Milei plans to implement a fiscal adjustment equivalent to 5% of GDP through cuts that primarily target the state rather than the private sector.

3. Despite the risks and challenges, Milei's shock therapy economic plan has gained support from investors and voters, who see him as their last hope for change.

Nobel Peace Prize winner vows Iranian people will ‘dismantle despotism’ in speech delivered by her children

-CNN

1. Imprisoned Iranian activist Narges Mohammadi's Nobel Peace Prize lecture, delivered by her children, highlighted the determination of the Iranian people to dismantle the regime.

2. Mohammadi described the impact of the "Woman, Life, Freedom" protests and emphasized the enduring struggle for change and freedom.

3. Mohammadi called on Western governments for more serious attention and effective strategies to empower the Iranian people and promote democracy in Iran.

U.S. Support for the Philippines in the South China Sea - United States Department of State

-Department of State

1. Chinese ships used water cannons and reckless maneuvers, causing damage to Philippine vessels and jeopardizing the safety of the Filipino crew.

2. Chinese ships also used acoustic devices to incapacitate Filipino crew members and drove away Philippine fishing vessels.

3. The actions of China obstructed supply lines and undermined regional stability, violating international law and a 2016 arbitral decision.

🏦 BUSINESS NEWS

Stock futures are slightly higher ahead of final Fed meeting of 2023: Live updates

-CNBC

1. U.S. stock futures are slightly higher as investors await the Federal Reserve meeting for signals on interest rate cuts.

2. The boom in equities is expected to continue, with investors becoming optimistic about further gains.

3. Consumer fears over inflation have declined, while consumer optimism has increased, according to a recent survey.

Macy's mulling $5.8 billion buyout offer: source

-Yahoo Finance

1. Macy's has received a $5.8 billion buyout offer from real estate investor Arkhouse Management and asset manager Brigade Capital Management.

2. The offer values Macy's at about $21 a share, marking a 32.4% premium to its closing price on Nov. 30.

3. Macy's board, led by retail veterans, is considering the offer while also evaluating the company's real estate holdings and potential disruption to the ongoing holiday shopping season.

Shari Redstone Considers Selling Her Stake in Paramount’s Parent Company

-The New York Times

1. Media mogul Shari Redstone is in talks to sell a controlling stake in National Amusements, the parent company of CBS, MTV, and Paramount movie studio.

2. Skydance, founded by David Ellison, is one of the potential buyers in talks with National Amusements.

3. The potential sale of Paramount could trigger significant changes in the media industry, with Comcast and Warner Bros. Discovery also actively seeking deals.

🤖 TECH NEWS

GTA 6 fan reimagines trailer for Switch port using PlayStation 1 graphics

-Dexerto

1. A GTA 6 fan recreated the game's announcement trailer in old-school PlayStation 1 graphics, joking that it would be the Nintendo Switch version.

2. The Nintendo Switch has been mocked for its numerous ported versions of popular AAA games, leading to skepticism about the performance of potential GTA 6 port.

3. A person claiming to be the real-life "Florida Joker" demanded payment from Rockstar for using his likeness in the GTA 6 trailer, but actor Roger Clark advised against it, stating that the company is experienced in handling such situations.

The new iPhone security threat that allows hackers to spy on your phone

-Fox News

1. Hackers are exploiting a vulnerability in iOS to spy on users through their keyboard.

2. They install a third-party keyboard with a keylogger into iPhones using Apple's TestFlight tool.

3. Users can protect themselves by checking their keyboard settings, avoiding unknown apps, and regularly updating their iOS.

Gemini Ultra, Google's new AI, could be smarter than OpenAI's GPT-4

-Business Insider

1. Google's Gemini AI model, specifically the Gemini Ultra, outperformed OpenAI's GPT-4 in various areas, including knowledge of subjects, code generation, and multi-step reasoning. Gemini Ultra scored 90% on the Massive Multitask Language Understanding test (MMLU), surpassing human expert performance.

2. Gemini Ultra is the first model to outperform human experts on the MMLU, a test that evaluates both world knowledge and problem-solving abilities. It achieved a score of 90%, compared to GPT-4's 86.4%.

3. Gemini has the advantage of being natively multimodal, designed to process various types of data including text, audio, code, images, and video. Its superior understanding of different inputs sets it apart from other multimodal models created by combining separate models for different data types.
