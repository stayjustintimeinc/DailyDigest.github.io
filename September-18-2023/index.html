🌎 WORLD NEWS

Everything you need to know about this year's meeting of leaders at the UN General Assembly

-The Associated Press

1. The 78th session of the UN General Assembly opens with no single clear crisis dominating the General Debate, but with ongoing wars, political crises, economic instability, and natural disasters.

2. The theme for this year's General Debate is "Rebuilding trust and reigniting global solidarity: Accelerating action on the 2030 Agenda and its Sustainable Development Goals towards peace, prosperity, progress, and sustainability for all."

3. While some key leaders will be absent, heads of state and government from at least 145 countries are expected to attend and address the General Assembly, including Brazilian President Lula da Silva, US President Joe Biden, and Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

Ukraine Dismisses All 6 Deputy Defense Ministers: Live Updates and News

-The New York Times

1. Ukraine dismisses all six deputy defense ministers in a major shake-up of its defense ministry.

2. The dismissals come as President Zelensky seeks to demonstrate tighter management of the defense ministry ahead of UN and US visits.

3. The move is seen as a positive step towards addressing corruption and reform in Ukraine's defense ministry.

Kim Jong Un returns to North Korea with gifts from Russia: Rifle, fur hat, drones

-NBC News

1. North Korean leader Kim Jong Un received several gifts from his hosts in Russia, including a Russian-made rifle, a cosmonaut's glove, and military drones, which violate U.N. sanctions.

2. The gifts also included modern body armor, attack drones, and a reconnaissance drone, which are all in violation of U.N. Security Council resolutions regarding North Korea's prohibited activities.

3. Kim also received a fur hat, a photo of Yuri Gagarin, and various other items for the International Friendship Exhibition, a museum dedicated to showcasing gifts received by North Korea's leaders from foreign dignitaries.

Around 64,000 portable generators recalled over burn injury risk

-NPR

1. Generac is recalling around 64,000 portable generators due to a fire and burn hazard, with at least three severe burn injuries reported.

2. The recall includes the GP15000E and GP17500E portable generator types, sold from April 2011 to June 2023, and consumers are urged to stop using them immediately.

3. Consumers can contact Generac for a free repair kit, but it may take six to eight weeks for the kit to arrive.

Intel Unveils Industry-Leading Glass Substrates to Meet Demand for More Powerful Compute

-Investor Relations :: Intel Corporation (INTC)

1. Intel has announced the development of glass substrates for advanced packaging, which will enable the scaling of transistors and advance Moore's Law for data-centric applications.

2. Glass substrates offer benefits such as ultra-low flatness and better thermal and mechanical stability, resulting in higher interconnect density in a substrate.

3. By the end of the decade, the semiconductor industry may reach limits with organic materials, making glass substrates a necessary next step for the next generation of semiconductors.

💡 Q&A OF THE DAY

What would happen if the laws of gravity suddenly disappeared?

The absence of gravity would result in chaos on Earth. Without gravity to hold everything down, objects, including people, would float aimlessly into the atmosphere. Buildings, vehicles, and even oceans would be launched into space, endangering all life forms. The lack of gravity would also affect the rotation of our planet, leading to disrupted weather patterns and extreme climate changes. Overall, the disappearance of gravity would have catastrophic consequences for our planet.
