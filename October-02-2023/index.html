🌎 WORLD NEWS

Two people and their dog killed in rare grizzly bear attack in Canada national park

-The Guardian

1. A couple, along with their dog, was killed in a rare grizzly bear attack in Canada's Banff national park.

2. The bear was demonstrating aggressive behavior and was subsequently killed by park rangers.

3. The fatalities mark the first in half a century within the border of Banff national park, and experts are investigating the incident to determine the cause.

Slovakia election winner Robert Fico opposes aid to Ukraine

-NPR

1. Slovakia's election winner Robert Fico, leader of the pro-Russian and anti-American Smer party, raised concerns as he campaigned to cease all military support for Ukraine.

2. Fico's promise to stop military aid to Ukraine may have limited impact as Slovakia has already exhausted much of its weaponry in the past two years.

3. Fico's potential government could have long-term implications for the European Union and NATO, potentially hindering Ukraine's path to joining these organizations. Additionally, concerns arise about the future of civil society and attacks on non-governmental organizations under Fico's leadership.

Kosovo attack: Who benefits? – POLITICO

-POLITICO Europe

1. The recent clash between armed Serbs and police in Kosovo's Zvečan municipality is perplexing, and it is worth questioning whose interests it serves.

2. Kosovo's leaders blame Serbian President Aleksandar Vučić for the attack, alleging that he planned and ordered it to destabilize the country and start a war.

3. However, Serbia denies these allegations and argues that carrying out an attack in Kosovo would go against their improving relations with the West and make no sense strategically.

Sam Bankman-Fried’s fraud trial is about to start. Here’s what you need to know

-CNN

1. Sam Bankman-Fried, the former crypto superstar, is standing trial for one of the biggest frauds in US history, with seven counts of fraud and conspiracy in connection with the collapse of his crypto-trading platform FTX.

2. Prosecutors allege that Bankman-Fried stole billions of dollars from FTX customer funds for his personal use and to cover losses incurred by Alameda Research, a crypto hedge fund he controlled. They also accuse him of defrauding investors in FTX by concealing the scheme.

3. FTX, which marketed itself as an easy and safe cryptocurrency trading platform, experienced a downfall in November 2022 when customers panicked and withdrew their funds due to financial concerns surrounding Alameda's assets. FTX filed for bankruptcy, and Bankman-Fried resigned as CEO before being arrested and extradited to the United States.

🤖 TECH NEWS

Microsoft CEO warns of ‘nightmare’ future for AI if Google’s search dominance continues

-CNN

1. Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella warns that allowing Google's dominance in online search to continue could lead to a "nightmare" scenario for the internet, especially in the emerging field of artificial intelligence.

2. Nadella highlights Google's agreements with companies like Apple, which make Google the default search engine, as barriers preventing access to rival search engines.

3. Nadella expresses concerns that Google's access to vast amounts of search data through its default agreements gives it an advantage in training its AI models, which could further strengthen its power and competitiveness.

Google's Chromebook Plus initiative pushes higher spec standards and handy software tools

-Engadget

1. Google is launching the Chromebook Plus initiative to provide consistent and high-quality Chromebooks with guaranteed specs, including Intel Core or AMD Ryzen processors, 8GB RAM, and 128GB storage.

2. Eight Chromebook Plus models are being announced in collaboration with HP, Acer, ASUS, and Lenovo, offering 14-inch screens and starting at $399, making them affordable options for most users.

3. Chromebook Plus devices will receive software improvements, such as a Material You design, dynamic wallpapers, Magic Eraser tool in Google Photos, and AI-powered enhancements for video calls. Google is also partnering with Adobe to offer a three-month trial of Photoshop on the web for Chromebook Plus buyers.

Pixel Watch 2 will have automatic workout tracking, new leak shows

-9to5Google

1. The first Pixel Watch released with Fitbit integration but had missing features and lacked automatic workout tracking.

2. The upcoming Pixel Watch 2 is rumored to include automatic workout tracking, along with new health features such as stress tracking and skin temperature readings.

3. Google is expected to launch the Pixel Watch 2 alongside the new Pixel 8 phones on October 4th.
