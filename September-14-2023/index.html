FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

Fun Fact: On this day, September 14th, in 1959, the Soviet Union's Luna 2 spacecraft became the first human-made object to reach the moon. It crash-landed on the moon's surface, marking the first successful impact on another celestial body. This achievement paved the way for future lunar missions and added to the ongoing space exploration race between the United States and the Soviet Union during the Cold War era.
