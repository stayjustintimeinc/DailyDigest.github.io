FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

On this day, December 24th, in 1968, the iconic Apollo 8 mission made history by becoming the first manned spacecraft to orbit the Moon. The crew members, astronauts Frank Borman, James Lovell, and William Anders, circled the Moon ten times and captured the iconic "Earthrise" photograph, showcasing our planet peeking over the lunar surface. This groundbreaking expedition paved the way for future Moon landings and marked a significant milestone in humanity's journey towards space exploration.

Housing affordability hit a new low in 2023

-NPR

1. Just 15.5% of homes for sale in the US were affordable for a typical household in 2023, the lowest share in at least a decade. Affordability dropped 40% from before the pandemic and 21% from the previous year, primarily due to spiking mortgage rates.

2. White households had more affordable options compared to Hispanic and Black households, with only 7% of listings being affordable for a typical Black household. There were significant geographic differences, with smaller cities experiencing the biggest drops in affordability.

3. The severe housing shortage in the US, coupled with high prices, is a major driver of record homelessness rates. While more cities and states are loosening zoning laws to encourage construction, it will take years to find a balance. However, there is some cause for optimism as inflation and mortgage rates have come down, and more people are expected to list their homes for sale after the holidays.

-CNN

1. Around 100 employees of an Airbus subsidiary in France fell ill after a food-borne illness outbreak at their company Christmas party.

2. The incident did not result in any serious illnesses, and all employees were back at work by the following week.

3. French health authorities are conducting an investigation to determine the cause of the illness outbreak and prevent future occurrences.
