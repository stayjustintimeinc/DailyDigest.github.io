FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

On this day in history, October 5th, 1969, the popular children's television show "Sesame Street" made its debut, captivating audiences with its educational and entertaining content. Created by Joan Ganz Cooney and Lloyd Morrisett, the show aimed to help underprivileged kids attain basic education through a combination of live-action actors, puppetry, animation, and catchy songs. Over the years, "Sesame Street" became an international sensation, reaching children and families worldwide in over 140 countries. The show has received numerous accolades and awards, becoming a beloved and iconic part of television history.

🌎 WORLD NEWS

Russian Attack in Kharkiv Region Kills Over 50, Ukraine Says: Live Updates

-The New York Times

1. Dozens of civilians attending a memorial service in the Kharkiv region of Ukraine were killed in a rocket strike, which President Zelensky condemned as a deliberate act of terrorism by Russia.

2. The attack in Hroza is one of the deadliest on civilians in months and highlights the ongoing conflict between Ukraine and Russia.

3. President Zelensky called for more air defense weapons and expressed confidence in continued U.S. support for Ukraine's war effort.

Injury of 16-year-old Iranian girl not wearing headscarf in Tehran sparks anger

-FRANCE 24 English

1. A 16-year-old girl in Iran was injured on a Metro train after reportedly not wearing a headscarf, sparking anger and calls for an independent investigation.

2. Activists abroad allege that the girl may have been pushed or attacked due to not wearing the hijab, and demand a UN fact-finding mission on Iran.

3. This incident comes as Iran is increasing enforcement of strict penalties for not wearing the required head covering, and amidst ongoing protests against repressive laws.

Tropical Storm Philippe: Threats of flash flooding increase for Maine

-WMTW Portland

1. Tropical Storm Philippe is expected to bring heavy rain and the potential for flooding to Maine over the weekend.

2. Philippe is currently located 470 miles away from Bermuda with 50 mph winds and is moving north at a speed of 12 mph.

3. As Philippe approaches Maine, it will interact with a developing rainstorm, potentially leading to enhanced rainfall and gusty winds in the state.

🏦 BUSINESS NEWS

FTX co-founder admits he and Sam Bankman-Fried committed fraud

-CNN

1. The trial of Sam Bankman-Fried, co-founder of FTX crypto exchange, is underway, with Gary Wang, a key witness, testifying about multiple financial crimes committed by both of them.

2. Prosecutors claim that Bankman-Fried orchestrated a scheme to steal from customers and defraud investors, with Wang's testimony corroborating these claims.

3. The close financial ties between FTX and Alameda Research are a central issue, with allegations that FTX deceived customers and hid special advantages given to Alameda. Defense attorneys have yet to cross-examine Wang or present their own witnesses.

Elon Musk's Twitter takeover being probed by SEC

-Reuters

1. Elon Musk, CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, is under investigation by the SEC over his acquisition of Twitter, which amounted to $44 billion.

2. The investigation focuses on whether Musk broke federal securities laws and made false statements regarding the Twitter deal.

3. The SEC has sought to compel Musk to testify, but he has raised objections and refused to appear, leading to a court filing against him.

Treasury-Bond Collapse Ranks Among the Worst Market Crashes in History

-Markets Insider

1. Bond-market sell-off causing yields to soar, resulting in significant losses for long-term Treasury bonds.

2. Losses on Treasury bonds similar to stock-market losses during previous market crashes.

3. Long-term Treasurys experiencing one of the most extreme bond-market meltdowns in history, surpassing losses seen in previous eras.

Wear OS 4 is coming to the original Pixel Watch 'later this year'

-9to5Google

1. Wear OS 4, the latest update for Google's wearable platform, is officially launching with the Pixel Watch 2, but Google has confirmed that the original Pixel Watch will also receive the update later this year.

2. The update brings new features such as the ability to transfer the watch to a new phone and enhanced animations for Tiles. Additionally, a collaboration with Samsung has introduced a new XML-based watch face format, making it easier for developers to create designs.

3. There is a possibility that Wear OS 4 will first launch as a beta program for Pixel Watch before a formal release. It is unclear when the update will be available for other Wear OS brands like Fossil.
