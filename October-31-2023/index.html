FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

On this day in history, October 31st, in 1941, Mount Rushmore National Memorial was completed after 14 years of work. The iconic sculpture features the faces of four U.S. presidents: George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt, and Abraham Lincoln, carved into the granite cliffs of South Dakota. The monumental project aimed to showcase the nation's history and pay tribute to these influential leaders. Today, Mount Rushmore continues to attract millions of visitors each year, offering a magnificent testament to American craftsmanship and artistry.
