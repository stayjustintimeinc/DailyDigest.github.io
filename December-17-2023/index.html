FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

On this day, December 17th, in 1903, the Wright brothers achieved their first successful powered flight in Kitty Hawk, North Carolina. Piloted by Orville Wright, their aircraft, the Wright Flyer, managed to stay airborne for a remarkable 12 seconds and covered a distance of 120 feet. This groundbreaking achievement marked the birth of modern aviation, paving the way for the incredible progress and innovations in air travel that we enjoy today!

Macy's Herald Square flagship, Bloomingdale's at 59th St. unlikely to undergo redevelopment

-New York Post

1. Hedge funds Brigade Capital Management and Arkhouse Management have made a bid of $21 per share to acquire Macy's Inc., aiming to capitalize on the department store giant's valuable real estate assets.

2. Macy's owns nearly half of its 500 US stores, including its flagship store Macy's Herald Square and Bloomingdale's on Lexington Avenue. However, it is unlikely that these properties will be redeveloped as Macy's had announced plans to build an office skyscraper on top of its flagship store before the takeover bid.

3. The potential new owners may instead focus on the Bloomingdale's site at 1000 Third Avenue, which is leased by Macy's until 2058. This site could potentially be suitable for a luxury condo tower rather than office space.
