FUN FACT ON THIS DAY:

On this day in history, October 26th, in 1881, the famous gunfight known as the "Gunfight at the O.K. Corral" took place in Tombstone, Arizona. This iconic event involved lawmen Wyatt Earp, his brothers Virgil and Morgan Earp, and friend Doc Holliday confronting a group of outlaws known as the Cochise County Cowboys. The gunfight lasted for about 30 seconds and ended with three outlaws killed and three lawmen injured. It remains a well-known and frequently romanticized event in the Wild West lore.
