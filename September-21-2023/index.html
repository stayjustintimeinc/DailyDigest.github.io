India suspends visas for Canadian nationals as diplomatic spat deepens

-CNN

1. India has suspended visa services for Canadian citizens due to "security threats" against diplomats in Canada, escalating tensions between the two countries.

2. BLS International, the agency handling visa applications for India in Canada, announced the suspension and stated it was due to "operation reasons."

3. The diplomatic row between India and Canada started when Canada accused India of potentially being involved in the assassination of a Sikh separatist activist, which India vehemently denied.

Ukraine war live updates: Russian barrage brings death, ruin, fury

-USA TODAY

1. At least five people were killed and dozens injured in missile and artillery strikes across Ukrainian cities, confirming President Volodymyr Zelenskyy's claim that Russia is a "terrorist state."

2. The Russian attack targeted both military and civilian infrastructure, causing significant damage in multiple areas.

3. Ukraine has secured a new shipping corridor in the Black Sea, and a cargo ship successfully arrived in Turkey with 3,000 tons of wheat.

Rupert Murdoch to Retire From Fox and News Corporation Boards

-The New York Times

1. Rupert Murdoch retires from the Fox and News Corporation boards, leaving his son Lachlan as the sole executive in charge of the media empire he built.

2. The retirement solidifies Lachlan's control but raises the possibility of a succession battle among Rupert Murdoch's four adult children after his death.

3. Despite retiring, Rupert Murdoch plans to remain actively involved in the company, offering counsel and participating in its future.

Real estate stocks slump after Fed signals higher for longer rate outlook (NYSEARCA:XLRE)

-Seeking Alpha

1. The real estate sector experienced a significant drop following the Federal Reserve's projection of higher interest rates for this year and the next, leading to increased financing costs for consumers and businesses in the real estate market.

2. The Real Estate Select Sector SPDR ETF (XLRE) fell by 3.1%, while real estate broker companies like Redfin and Compass saw substantial declines in their stock prices.

3. Homebuilders and mortgage-related investment trusts also suffered as higher mortgage rates affected home affordability, resulting in declines in companies such as Taylor Morrison Home, KB Home, and New York Mortgage Trust.
