A pod of orcas have sunk a yacht in the Strait of Gibraltar

-NPR

1. A pod of orcas has been repeatedly ramming boats in the waters separating southern Europe and northern Africa, causing major damage.

2. Since 2020, there have been about 500 encounters between orcas and boats, leading to at least three boat sinkings.

3. Researchers have proposed theories that the orcas may be playing or seeking the feel of the rudder, while others suggest it could be an act of revenge for previous traumatic encounters with fishing boats.

-KOMO News

3. The Titanic sank in 1912, resulting in the loss of 1,517 lives, and the wreck was discovered in 1985.

How WeWork Rose and Went Broke - WSJ

-The Wall Street Journal

1. WeWork, a co-working space startup, has filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy after investors were optimistic about its potential to become the next Facebook.

2. The downfall of WeWork serves as a lesson on how easy money can contribute to speculative investments.

3. WeWork was founded by Adam Neumann in 2010, aiming to lease office spaces long-term and sublease them to startups at higher rates, while providing millennial amenities.

Stock market news today: Stocks extend win streak as investors cling to Fed rate excitement

-Yahoo Finance

1. Stocks continue to rise for the seventh straight session as investors gain confidence in the Federal Reserve ending its tightening campaign.

2. The tech-heavy Nasdaq Composite and the benchmark S&P 500 both saw gains, with the Nasdaq rising by 0.9% and the S&P increasing by nearly 0.3%.

3. Despite the market's optimism, cautious voices, including Minneapolis Fed President Neel Kashkari, emphasize that the central bank may still have more work to do to control inflation.

Stellantis says 2025 Ram 1500 Ramcharger hybrid will appeal to EV skeptics

-Fox Business

1. Stellantis has officially introduced the 2025 Ram 1500 Ramcharger pickup truck, which is touted as the "pinnacle of the light-duty pickup truck segment and the ultimate electric truck."

2. The 2025 Ram 1500 Ramcharger is equipped with a 92-kilowatt-hour battery pack, a 130-kilowatt generator, and a 3.6-liter engine that powers the generator. It can charge the battery when depleted or preserve battery power.

3. The truck has a targeted range of up to 690 miles and offers features such as towing capacity of up to 14,000 pounds, DC fast-charging, bi-directional charging, and digital key support.
